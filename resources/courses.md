# Online Courses and Tutorials

Curated list of online courses, video lectures, and interactive tutorials for learning quantum mechanics and quantum computing.

---

## University Courses (Free Online)

### MIT OpenCourseWare

**8.04: Quantum Physics I**
- **Level:** Undergraduate
- **Instructor:** Allan Adams
- **Platform:** MIT OCW
- **Content:** Full course with videos, notes, problem sets
- **Best for:** Systematic introduction to quantum mechanics
- **URL:** ocw.mit.edu

**8.05: Quantum Physics II**
- **Level:** Undergraduate (continuation)
- **Content:** Advanced topics, approximation methods
- **Best for:** Deeper dive after basics

**8.370x: Quantum Information Science (edX)**
- **Level:** Graduate
- **Instructor:** Peter Shor, Isaac Chuang, et al.
- **Focus:** Quantum information and computation
- **Best for:** Comprehensive quantum computing course

### Stanford Online

**Quantum Mechanics for Scientists and Engineers**
- **Platform:** Stanford Online / edX
- **Instructor:** David Miller
- **Level:** Graduate
- **Focus:** Practical quantum mechanics
- **Best for:** Engineers and applied scientists

**Leonard Susskind's Theoretical Minimum**
- **Platform:** YouTube / Stanford Continuing Studies
- **Series:** "Quantum Mechanics"
- **Level:** Beginner to Intermediate
- **Format:** Full lecture series
- **Best for:** Building from ground up with minimal prerequisites

### Caltech

**Ph219: Quantum Computation (John Preskill)**
- **Platform:** Lecture notes online, some videos
- **Level:** Graduate
- **Focus:** Comprehensive quantum computation
- **Best for:** Serious students of quantum computing
- **URL:** theory.caltech.edu/~preskill/ph229/

### UC Berkeley

**Quantum Mechanics and Quantum Computation (Umesh Vazirani)**
- **Platform:** YouTube
- **Level:** Graduate
- **Focus:** Computer science perspective
- **Best for:** CS students entering quantum computing

## Self-Study Recommendations

### 3-Month Intensive Plan

**Month 1: Foundations**
- Susskind videos (1-2 lectures/week)
- Khan Academy Linear Algebra
- IBM Quantum Learning basics

**Month 2: Quantum Mechanics**
- MIT 8.04 lectures
- Qiskit Textbook Ch 1-3
- Work problems from textbook

**Month 3: Quantum Computing**
- Vazirani lectures
- Qiskit algorithms tutorials
- Build small projects

### 6-Month Comprehensive Plan

**Months 1-2:** Math + QM foundations
**Months 3-4:** Quantum computing theory
**Months 5-6:** Specialization (algorithms/hardware/applications)

### Weekend Quickstart

**Saturday:**
- Watch: 3Blue1Brown quantum video
- Read: IBM Quantum Learning intro
- Do: First Qiskit tutorial

**Sunday:**
- Watch: 2-3 Susskind lectures
- Read: Qiskit Textbook Ch 1
- Do: Build first quantum circuit

## Tips for Online Learning

1. **Active learning:** Don't just watch - do exercises
2. **Pace yourself:** Quantum concepts need time to sink in
3. **Multiple resources:** Different explanations help
4. **Community:** Join forums, ask questions
5. **Projects:** Build something to cement understanding
6. **Regular schedule:** Consistent study beats cramming
7. **Notes:** Write summaries in your own words
